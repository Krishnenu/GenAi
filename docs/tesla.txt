Tesla Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company founded in 2003.

Tesla's CEO is Elon Musk.

Tesla designs and manufactures:
- Electric Cars
- Battery Energy Storage Systems
- Solar Panels
- Solar Roof Products

Popular Tesla vehicle models include:
- Model S
- Model 3
- Model X
- Model Y
- Cybertruck

Tesla's headquarters is located in Austin, Texas, United States.

Tesla focuses on sustainable energy and autonomous driving technologies.

Tesla's Full Self-Driving (FSD) system uses artificial intelligence and computer vision techniques to assist drivers.
